IFRS 7 Risk Reporting. Simple.

IFRS7.org is a risk reporting platform built by John Orford and his team to make disclosure easier, more accurate, and reconcilable.

Get productive with IFRS7.org.

Features.

IFRS7.org reports the nature and extent of risks to your portfolio arising from financial instruments as specified by IFRS*.

Market Risk Analysis

A sensitivity analysis of each market risk factor.

Liquidity

Breakdown by timing of cash flows.

Credit

Analytical disclosure of financial asset credit quality.

Concentration

Identify asset allocation over-weighting.

* See this list of disclosures on the IFRS website.
